--- title: Sliding Puzzle Generator layout: project posted: 04-07-15 updated: 04-30-15 publisher: Corbin Steele version: 1.3.0.0 image: /img/sliding-puzzle-moon.png link: Sliding Puzzle Generator.application netversion: 4.5.0 --- A simple program that allows you to generate your own sliding puzzle game, specifying custom dimensions and any image from your own computer for the game. Has multiple options to customize the appearance of the game, a sliding tile randomizer that allows you to watch as the game shuffles itself before play, and a reset feature.
